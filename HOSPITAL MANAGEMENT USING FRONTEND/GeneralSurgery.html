About Our General Surgery Services

Our General Surgery unit manages a wide range of surgical conditions with emphasis on evidence-based practice, minimally invasive techniques and fast recovery protocols.

  • Emergency and elective abdominal surgery
  • Advanced laparoscopic and endoscopic procedures
  • Breast, colorectal and hernia surgery
  • Trauma, wound care and surgical oncology collaborations

Treatments & Procedures

Acute & Emergency Surgery

Management of appendicitis, intestinal obstruction, perforation and traumatic abdominal injuries.

Laparoscopic Surgery

Cholecystectomy, hernia repair, diagnostic laparoscopy and complex minimally invasive procedures.

Hernia & Abdominal Wall

Open and laparoscopic hernia repair, complex abdominal wall reconstruction.

Breast & Soft Tissue

Breast lump evaluation, biopsy, lumpectomy and oncoplastic care pathways.

Colorectal Care

Management of diverticular disease, inflammatory bowel complications, colorectal cancer surgery and stoma care.

Diagnostics & Preoperative Work-up

Complete pre-op assessment and on-site imaging and labs to optimise surgical planning and patient safety.

  • Ultrasound, CT and MRI for surgical planning
  • Endoscopy and colonoscopy services
  • Preoperative labs, cardiopulmonary evaluation and anesthetic assessment
  • Wound and drainage management

Perioperative Support & Rehabilitation

Enhanced recovery after surgery (ERAS), physiotherapy, stoma counselling and nutrition support for faster recovery.
