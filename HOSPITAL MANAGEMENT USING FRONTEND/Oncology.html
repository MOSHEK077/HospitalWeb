About Our Oncology Care

We provide multidisciplinary oncology care using evidence-based protocols, tumour boards for personalised plans and supportive care to optimise outcomes and quality of life.

  • Diagnosis, staging and personalised treatment planning
  • Medical oncology (chemotherapy, targeted therapy, immunotherapy)
  • Surgical oncology and oncoplastic procedures
  • Radiation oncology including IMRT and image-guided RT

Treatments & Services

Systemic Therapy

IV and oral chemotherapy, targeted agents and immunotherapy with ambulatory infusion suites and monitoring.

Surgical Oncology

Oncologic resections, sentinel lymph node biopsy, minimally invasive and reconstructive procedures.

Radiation Therapy

Advanced modalities: IMRT, IGRT, 3D-conformal RT and palliative radiotherapy options.

Bone Marrow & Hemato-Oncology

Evaluation and treatment of hematologic malignancies; referral pathways for transplant when indicated.

Palliative & Supportive Care

Symptom management, pain control, nutrition, psychosocial support and end-of-life care planning.

Diagnostics & Molecular Testing

Comprehensive diagnostic services including imaging, pathology and molecular profiling to personalise therapy.

  • Biopsy, histopathology and immunohistochemistry
  • CT, MRI, PET-CT for staging and response assessment
  • Molecular and genetic testing for targeted therapies
  • Tumour board case review for multidisciplinary planning

Patient Support & Survivorship

Nutritional counselling, psycho-oncology, rehabilitation, survivorship planning and an insurance assistance desk to streamline care.
