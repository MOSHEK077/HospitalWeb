About Our ENT Care

Our ENT team diagnoses and treats disorders of the ear, nose, throat, sinuses, voice and balance using clinical expertise and modern technology.

  • Medical and surgical management of ENT conditions
  • Hearing, balance and voice disorder evaluation
  • Head & neck surgery including thyroid and salivary gland care

Treatments & Services

Ear Care & Hearing

Otitis management, ear microsurgery, hearing tests and hearing aid/cochlear implant referrals.

Sinus & Nasal Surgery

Endoscopic sinus surgery, septoplasty and management of allergies and polyps.

Throat & Voice

Assessment and treatment for hoarseness, tonsillitis, swallowing disorders and voice therapy.

Head & Neck Oncology

Diagnosis and surgical management of head & neck tumors with multidisciplinary care.

Balance & Vestibular Clinic

Evaluation and rehabilitation for vertigo and balance disorders.

Diagnostics & Audiology

Full ENT diagnostic support including audiology, endoscopy and imaging to guide personalised care plans.

  • Audiometry, tympanometry and ABR testing
  • Nasal endoscopy and laryngoscopy
  • CT/MRI for sinus, skull base and neck evaluation
  • Voice/video stroboscopy for vocal cord assessment

Patient Support & Rehabilitation

Hearing aid services, speech & language therapy, vestibular rehab and counselling for chronic ENT conditions.
