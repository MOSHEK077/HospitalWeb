About Our Gynecology Care

We provide holistic, evidence-based care for women at all life stages including antenatal, intrapartum and postnatal services, minimally invasive gynecologic surgery and reproductive health.

  • Antenatal, delivery and postnatal care including high-risk pregnancy management
  • Minimally invasive gynecologic surgery (laparoscopy, hysteroscopy)
  • Menstrual disorder and pelvic pain management
  • Family planning, fertility evaluation and preconception counselling

Treatments & Services

Pregnancy & Maternity Care

Regular antenatal clinics, ultrasound surveillance, labor & delivery services and postpartum support.

Minimally Invasive Surgery

Laparoscopy and hysteroscopy for fibroids, endometriosis, ovarian cysts and diagnostic procedures.

Menopause & Hormonal Care

Symptom management, bone health evaluation and individualized hormone therapy where appropriate.

Family Planning & Contraception

Comprehensive counselling and provision of contraceptive options including IUDs and implants.

Colposcopy & Cancer Screening

Cervical screening, HPV counselling, colposcopy and referral pathways for oncology care.

Diagnostics & Fetal Medicine

On-site imaging and laboratory support including advanced fetal ultrasound and prenatal screening services.

  • Obstetric ultrasound, anomaly scan and doppler studies
  • Pelvic ultrasound, hysterosonography
  • Hormonal and endocrine testing, STI screening
  • Colposcopy, Pap smear and HPV testing

Patient Support & Education

Ante-natal classes, breastfeeding support, pelvic floor physiotherapy and counselling for reproductive choices and menopausal care.

24/7 rapid response for obstetric emergencies (bleeding, pre-eclampsia, obstruction), ectopic pregnancy, torsion and acute pelvic pain. Immediate triage and specialist intervention available.
