About Our Urology Care

We manage kidney stones, urinary tract infections, prostate disorders, bladder dysfunction and urologic cancers using modern diagnostics and minimally invasive techniques.

  • Stone disease: extracorporeal lithotripsy, ureteroscopy and PCNL
  • Endourology and minimally invasive urologic surgery
  • Benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH) management
  • Urologic oncology: kidney, bladder, prostate & testicular cancers
  • Andrology & male infertility evaluation

Treatments & Services

Stone Management

ESWL, flexible ureteroscopy, and percutaneous nephrolithotomy with perioperative metabolic evaluation.

Minimally Invasive Surgery

Laparoscopic and robotic-assisted procedures for complex reconstructive and oncologic cases.

BPH & Voiding Dysfunction

Medical therapy, TURP and modern minimally invasive prostate procedures.

Urologic Oncology

Diagnosis, surgery, systemic therapy coordination and survivorship support.

Andrology & Infertility

Semen analysis, varicocele repair, microsurgical sperm retrieval and counselling.

Diagnostics & Imaging

On-site imaging and specialised tests for accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.

  • Urine culture, cytology and metabolic stone work-up
  • Ultrasound KUB, Doppler and pelvic ultrasound
  • CT urography and MRI for complex cases
  • Urodynamics and uroflowmetry for bladder function assessment

Patient Support & Rehabilitation

Pre- and post-op counselling, continence rehabilitation, pain management and an assistance desk for insurance and appointments.
