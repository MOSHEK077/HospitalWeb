About Our Pediatrics Care

Our Pediatric team provides age-appropriate, family-centred care covering newborn care, routine immunisation, acute illness management and long-term conditions.

  • Neonatal care & NICU support
  • Well-baby checks, growth & developmental monitoring
  • Immunisation and vaccine counselling
  • Management of common childhood illnesses and chronic pediatric conditions

Services & Programs

Newborn & NICU Care

Thermal care, feeding support, jaundice management and ventilatory support when required.

Immunisation Clinic

Routine vaccine schedule, catch-up immunisations and vaccine counselling for parents.

Acute Pediatric Care

Evaluation and treatment for fever, respiratory infections, gastroenteritis and minor injuries.

Chronic Care & Follow-up

Asthma, epilepsy, diabetes and other long-term pediatric conditions with multidisciplinary clinics.

Diagnostics & Nutrition

On-site pediatric lab tests, ultrasound, basic imaging and nutritional assessment to support diagnosis and growth plans.

  • Complete blood tests, cultures & point-of-care testing
  • Chest X-ray and abdominal ultrasound
  • Growth charts, BMI-for-age and micronutrient screening

Family Support & Education

Parental counselling, breastfeeding support, vaccination reminders and school health advice to empower families.

Breastfeeding & Lactation Support
Child Development Screening
Nutrition & Growth Clinics
Immunisation Records & Reminders

Pediatric Emergency Care

24/7 pediatric emergency team for breathing difficulty, high fever, seizures, severe dehydration and trauma. Rapid triage and stabilization for infants and children.
