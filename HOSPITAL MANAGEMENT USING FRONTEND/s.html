About Our Orthopedics Care

Our Orthopedics team treats bone, joint, muscle and spine conditions using evidence-based medicine, minimally invasive surgery, and multidisciplinary rehabilitation to restore function and quality of life.

  • Trauma and fracture management (including polytrauma).
  • Joint replacement: hip, knee, and shoulder arthroplasty.
  • Arthroscopy for knee, shoulder, and ankle injuries.
  • Spine surgery for degenerative and traumatic conditions.
  • Sports medicine, pediatric orthopedics, and diabetic foot care.

Diagnostics & Imaging

Accurate diagnosis is essential. We offer on-site imaging and functional testing for rapid assessment and planning.

  • X-ray & fluoroscopy for fracture and alignment assessment.
  • MRI for soft-tissue and ligament evaluation.
  • CT scans for complex fractures and pre-op planning.
  • DEXA scan for bone density & osteoporosis screening.
  • Ultrasound-guided injections and musculoskeletal ultrasound.
